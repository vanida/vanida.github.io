About

Hi there.

Here's where I talk about myself in the third person. Here goes. Vanida is an Emmy Award-winning storyteller, animator, and design enthusiast. She is a graduate of the School of the Art Institute of Chicago and has 9 years of industry experience in broadcast and advertising. Her credits include work for PBS, the City Colleges of Chicago, Daily Planet Productions and Groupon. Currently she serves as Creative Director for BrightBytes, an education technology startup in San Francisco, where she continues to geek out over UI & UX design, data visualization, illustration, and motion graphics.
